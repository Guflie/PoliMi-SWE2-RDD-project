\paragraph{[UC1] Account creation}
\begin{center}
    \begin{tabular}{|c|c|}
        \hline
        Name & \textbf{Account creation} \\
        \hline
        Actors & Any person \\
        \hline
        Entry Condition & \\
        \hline
        Event Flow & 
            \begin{minipage}{0.7\textwidth}
            \smallskip
            \begin{enumerate}
                \item The person opens the account creation pagebreak
                \item The system asks to fill out a form with the following personal information: name, surname, age, birth date, geneder, email; the form has also a flag for privacy accpetance
                \item The person fills out the questionare and submits it
                \item The system verifies that the email exists and sends a verification email containing a link to verify the email address
                \item The person receives the email and opens the link, which lead to a page in which it can type the password for the account
                \item The person types the password and submits the form
            \end{enumerate}
            \smallskip
            \end{minipage}
        \\
        \hline
        Exit Condition & The account is successfully created\\
        \hline
        Exception & Email inserted during registration has been already used\\
        \hline       
    \end{tabular}
\end{center}

We highlight that the same person can create multiple accounts, but with different emails.
